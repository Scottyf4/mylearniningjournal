Hi there! My name is Scott and welcome to my learning journal.

After years of wanting to learn and playing around with different online bootcamps and training courses I finally feel settled with Scrimba. The interactive learning and on screen video / typing and muscle memory learning has really helped develop my skills.

How I stay commited to Learning

My desire to learn seems to be growing stronger and stronger as I pass through the Frontend End Developer course with Scrimba. The modules are taught really well by great instructors and the community is great. Having a brilliant Discord community to share my accoumplishments with and get coding reviews is a HUGE advantage. The feeling you get when you complete a solo project and then upload it for the community to see if great, and when you get your first review it feels fantastic and is such a bonus to help you look back at your own code that you made and then see how you can implemented the recommendations made is amazing.
