About my journey

After years of broken learning I finally feel like I am making headway with my Frontend Developer journey. I started thinking about coding many years ago, and they journey has been nothing but smooth.

How I got Into Coding

I have always had an interest in learning new things, and one day watched a Youtube video on web Design. It seemed like something that really interested me. After doing my research I stumble upon some coding Bootcamps on Udemy and started to work my way through them. It wasnt until I found Scrimba, that I truly fell in love with all things Web Design. From here I have been following the Front End Developer Careeer Path and Intend to see this through to completion. I am really looking forward to the sections on React and API's.

How I stay ontop of my learning

This is sometimes harder than you may thing. Beinga husband and a father of 4, finding time to switch off to the world and concentrate on code is difficult. The Scrimba course and its community keep me wanting to learn and develop. The drive to understand how things work and make beautiful designs is what is really driving me, and even after a long shift at my full time work, I want to learn more and grow as a developer.
